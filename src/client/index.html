Help

Coursery helps you use course evaluations to find great classes. For example:

  • Find the highest rated classes in a department. Select Average for year, section, and instructor, the department for subject, and All for course. Click on the course rating to sort by that field.
  • Find the highest rated instructors for a class. Select Average for year and section. Choose the appropriate subject and course number. Select All for instructor. Click on the instructor rating to sort by that field.
  • See what ratings looked like for a class you took. Select the appropriate year, subject, course number, and section. Look at the summary data, or click the full report button (on the far right) to see the full evaluation.

Where did all this data come from? Students like you. These course evaluations are available on BannerWeb as well, but are simply presented here in a much more accessible way.
